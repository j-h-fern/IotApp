Iot Applications



What is an IoT Application

Iot is the idea that by using the internet and its ability to transport data
we can interconnect smart sensing devices and systems with more powerful data
storage and manipulation systems. This network can be used then to remotely
operate and monitor these devices and systems. This gives massive real time
analytical capacity and allows for better efficiency and more control.

Smart House

A smart house main feature would be been capable of centrally controlling and
manipulating various smart systems with in a domestic situation. With the aim of
maximising the automation of these systems an the associated efficiency
and benefits.

IoT Interface

Inter faces requierd in smart and IoT applications will be varied and unique
to each situation most will requier secure interfaces to avoid tampering and
others will have high levels of manual control.
